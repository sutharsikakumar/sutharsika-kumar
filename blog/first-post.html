Taking Initiative

30 March 2026

In a world where it's easier than ever to generate content, original thinking can easily be muddled. So, I hope to use this space to explore my own ideas, reflect on areas where I can grow, and open a space for thoughtful discussion on the issues and perspectives that shape our lives. Things such as how societies are structured, the future of tech, limitations in our own perceptions, etc. Having always been an occasional journal-er, I've come to believe that shaping those thoughts into essays is most likely going to be a fruitful action. Anyone who knows me in person would agree that I talk a lot, so writing in this way feels especially natural. Over time, I hope to build a collection of work that reflects how my understanding of the world changes with new experiences and explore some parts of my identity that I can't yet put into words. I also hope to inspire a younger generation of creative thinkers to use their words intentionally, to explore their intellectual curiosity and preserve their unique voice in a world where content is abundant, but depth is rare. In accordance with this goal, I hope to keep my essays somewhat raw.

With love and until next time,
Sutharsika Kumar
